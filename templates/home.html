FAQ

What's this?
A service that automatically charges $0.50 to your card once a quarter. Nothing more, nothing less.
Why?
It makes sense to keep unused credit cards with no annual fee open, however without any activity, they risk closure. Charging a small amount every month keeps activity on the card, thus preventing it from being closed.
Can I get the charged amount back?
Not at the moment. Since many issuers refund small balances though, you might not be billed for it. An auto-refund feature is planned for the future.
Can I change the amount or the have it charged yearly?
Not at the moment, but I'll add an option to change these settings if I get enough requests for them.
Is my credit card information secure?
All information is processed through Stripe and is not stored on our servers.
How do I contact you?
Email us at help@cardkeepalive.com.
